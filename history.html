History

The game of poker was created in the U.S. in the early 1800's. The game has some similarities to a Persian game named As-Nas, but has similarities to multiple different games. The game developed over tme and new rules and conventions were developed. Poker gained world wide popularity in the 1970's due in part to the founding of the World Series Of Poker(WSOP). Poker contiued to gain steadily in popularity until the early 2000's, when poker had an enormous spike in popularity due mostly to online poker and more televised tournaments.
